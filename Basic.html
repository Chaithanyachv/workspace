A full stack developer is a professional who works on both the front end
and back end of web development. They design, develop, and maintain platforms
that include databases or servers. The front end is the part of a website that
users see and interact with, while the back end is the data storage and processing
that happens behind the scenes. Full stack developers may work on projects involving APIs,
databases, building user-facing websites, or even working with clients during the planning phase of projects.
